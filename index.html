Free Fire Texture Modding Guide

1 Locate Original File

Connect your Android device to a PC or use a file manager. Navigate to:
Android/data/com.dts.freefireth/files/ImageCache/ Find the skin or texture file you want to replace (usually ends in .astc or .bin).

2 Patch & Inject

Upload that game file here as the Target File. Upload your custom PNG/JPG skin as the Replacement Image. Let Gemini AI analyze the offsets, then click Inject & Patch.

3 Replace & Play

Download the patched file from this tool. Rename it to match the exact original filename, and copy it back to the same directory on your phone. Restart Free Fire to see your custom skin!
